Neopolitan Icecream

Recipe Ingredients:

chocolate/chocolate chips

strawberry

2 cups heavy whipping cream

2 cups half-and-half cream

Instructions

Combine all ingredients, stirring to dissolve sugar completely.

Fill cylinder of ice cream maker no more than two-thirds full; freeze according to manufacturer's directions. (Refrigerate any remaining mixture until ready to freeze.) Serve immediately or store in covered containers in freezer.

Enjoy!
